# Leap Year

**Time Limit:** 1 second  
**Memory Limit:** 64 MB  
**Input:** standard input or `input.txt`  
**Output:** standard output or `output.txt`  

Determine whether a given year is a leap year according to the Gregorian calendar. A year divisible by 400 is a leap year. Other years divisible by 100 are not leap years (e.g., 1700, 1800, 1900, 2100, 2200, 2300). Other years divisible by 4 are leap years. All remaining years are not leap years.  

**Input:** A positive four-digit integer â€” the year number.  
**Output:** Print `YES` if the year is a leap year, and `NO` otherwise.  

**Example 1**  
Input: `2003`  
Output: `NO`  

**Example 2**  
Input: `2004`  
Output: `YES`  

**Example 3**  
Input: `3000`  
Output: `NO`
